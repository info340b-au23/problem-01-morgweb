August

Character in Taylor Swift's Album Folklore

The fictional character August (Augustine) is a character produced by Taylor Swift in her album Folklore specifically the song August. August is a girl who had a summer thing with another fictional character in Folklore named James. She is perceived to be this carefree girl who goes with the flow however in the song she is revealed to be a sensitive person who feels emotions immensely and ends up falling in love with James. She is a relatable character in this story/song because she takes a spot on the sidelines to watch the person she loves fall for someone else after she gave everything to this summer fling.

A summer fling and nothing more. “August girl,” filled with false hope and innocence, wished for more, a two-sided relationship that he could never give her.
